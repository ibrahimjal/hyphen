Design ———
A kitchen staple, reimagined
Hyphen is built to help busy kitchens stay one step ahead and spend more time on what matters most.
Do double duty
Hyphen is versatile, allowing orders to be prepared manually up top or assembling them auto­mat­i­cal­ly below with robotics.
Make it easy
Hyphen was designed to be used by anyone. Its user interface is simple and intuitive, like a smartphone, so you can train employees fast.
Put hospitality first
Hospitality and culinary creativity are core to foodservice. Hyphen takes robotic work out of the kitchen so staff can focus on what matters most.

Integrated Quality Control
Hyphen is monitored by thousands of sensors and uses AI-based vision to ensure every order is produced perfectly, with accurate portions and a new standard of quality control.
Lids and Labels
Hyphen lids and labels every meal with the customer’s name and meal info, so there’s no mix-up. Meals are made completely contactless.
Backsplash Display
Hyphen monitors each ingredient down to the gram and notifies staff when a hotel pan needs to be replenished. It also checks and maintains the set temperature, so your food is always safe.
Perfect Plating
Hyphen’s conveyor has the ability to dispense in a radial pattern — eliminating an ugly heap of ingredients and giving customers a snap-worthy plate every time.
Scalability ———
All the
ingredients you
need to scale
Hyphen modules are designed to grow with your business. Add, swap, or replace modules with ease.
Available modules
  • 6, 10 & 18 Hotel Pan Cabinets Build a custom makeline to fit your menu with three cabinet options, offering a range of hotel pan sizes and configurations.
  • Tray denesting module Auto­mat­i­cal­ly dispenses a bowl or plate, so orders keep flowing.
  • Mixing module Create the perfect dis­tri­b­u­tion of ingredients for salads and bowls — without the mess.
  • Portion cup fill module Precisely fill dressings and sauces into portion cups no matter how thick or thin.
  • Retherm module Quickly heat pre-cooked ingredients to exact specifications.
  • Beverage module Dispense beverages right as they’re needed, so orders move out the door quickly.
  • Lid & Label module Auto­mat­i­cal­ly secure lids and labels with the customer’s name and meal info.
  • Staging module Consolidate multiple menu items in a single order and stage orders based on pick-up or delivery.
HyphenOS ———
Total control at
your fingertips
Hyphen connects the dots of your data in real-time — and over time — providing actionable insights for better forecasting.
See it all
HyphenOS lets you track unit-level store performance, purchase inventory based on historical and real-time data, and develop new and seasonal recipes that can be easily synced to all your Hyphen makelines.
Streamline your workflow
HyphenOS brings a simpler, more customized workflow to your business and SOPs. It allows anyone on staff to easily manage and modify orders in seconds.
Pan in, pan out
HyphenOS monitors hotel pans to ensure ingredients are maintained at the right temperature and nudges staff when they’re running low. Minimize waste by auto­mat­i­cal­ly managing inventory and optimizing purchasing.
